About ITC Beauty Training

Empowering future beauticians with professional training, modern techniques, and real industry experience.

Professional Beauty Training Institute

ITC Beauty Training Institute is dedicated to providing high-quality beauty education for aspiring beauticians and makeup artists. With experienced trainers and practical learning environments, students gain real industry skills that prepare them for professional careers.

Our programs cover a wide range of beauty disciplines including hair styling, skincare, makeup artistry, and salon management. Over the years, we have helped hundreds of students develop the confidence and skills needed to succeed in the beauty industry.

Founder & Director

Shobha – Beauty Educator & Industry Expert

With years of professional experience in the beauty industry, Shobha founded ITC Beauty Training with a vision to empower aspiring beauticians with practical knowledge and industry-ready skills.

Her mission is to make professional beauty education accessible, practical, and career-focused. Through hands-on training and mentorship, she has helped hundreds of students start successful careers in salons, freelance makeup artistry, and beauty entrepreneurship.
