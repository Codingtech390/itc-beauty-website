Course Overview

This intensive Professional Makeup & Beauty Artistry course provides comprehensive training in foundational to advanced makeup techniques, color theory, product knowledge, and creative application. You will learn to create flawless looks for bridal, editorial, fashion, HD camera, airbrush, and special effects — while building a professional portfolio and mastering client consultation for real-world success in salons, studios, or freelance.

What You Will Master

  • ✔ Skin preparation, color theory & undertone matching
  • ✔ Contouring, highlighting, sculpting & face mapping
  • ✔ Eye makeup artistry (smokey, cut-crease, graphic liner)
  • ✔ Bridal, party, editorial & red-carpet makeup
  • ✔ HD & airbrush makeup techniques
  • ✔ Client consultation, hygiene protocols & portfolio development
